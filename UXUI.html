User Experience & User Interface?

What is User Experience & User Interface?

User Experience and User Interface, also known as UX/UI, are crucial in design. UX design focuses on the overall user journey and satisfaction with a product or service, while UI design concentrates on the visual elements and interactions within that product or service.

Job Roles

In the world of UX/UI design, there are numerous roles one can pursue after receiving their bachelor's degree or associate's degree. Some of these include: UX/UI Designers, Product Designers, UX Researchers, Information Architects, Interaction Designers, Usability Specialists, UX Managers, UX Strategists, Content Designers, Front-End Web Developers, and Motion Graphics Designers.
